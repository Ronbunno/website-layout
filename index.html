About

The largest Laravel event of the year
streaming September 14th on YouTube.

Once again we'll be streaming Laracon for free on YouTube allowing us to reach
the entire Laravel community. A huge “THANK YOU” to our incredible partners for
helping us achieve this!
All talks are brand new, never before presented at any Laracon and delivered live
during the event. We have 18 amazing speakers lined up including a keynote from
Taylor Otwell on the state of Laravel and what's new in Laravel.
